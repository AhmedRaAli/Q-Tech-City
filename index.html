Welcome to the Smart City of Qena

Learn More About The Smart City

The Smart City in Qena is a big step toward the future. Its main goal is to make life easier and faster for people by using modern technology in everyday services. Government services will be digital, so people can complete paperwork online without waiting in long lines. There are also smart ideas like automatic street lighting, smart traffic systems, and waste management to keep the city clean, safe, and organized. All of this helps save time and energy, improves daily life, and protects the environment
